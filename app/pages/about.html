About Fresh Share

Building stronger communities through food sharing, one neighborhood at a time.

Our Mission

At FreshShare, we're on a mission to transform how communities access fresh, quality food. By connecting neighbors and local producers through our platform, we're building a more sustainable and equitable food system.

We believe that by working together, we can make healthy food more affordable, support local producers, and build stronger communities - one group purchase at a time.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and strengthen community bonds by creating a platform where neighbors can share fresh, surplus food with each other.

Our Vision

A world where no food goes to waste, and communities are connected through the sharing of fresh, healthy food resources.

Our Values

Community, Sustainability, Generosity, and Innovation drive everything we do at Fresh Share.
