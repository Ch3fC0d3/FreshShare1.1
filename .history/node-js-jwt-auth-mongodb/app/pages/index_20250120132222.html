Understanding Fresh Share

A Fresh Share group is a collective of neighbors who pool their resources to purchase fresh, local food directly from farmers and suppliers. This collaborative approach enables members to access high-quality products at reduced prices while supporting local communities.

How Fresh Share Works

  • Group Ordering: Members express interest in local, fresh products, and once enough people join, the group places a bulk order directly from local farmers.
  • Cost Sharing: The total cost is divided among participants based on their orders, resulting in significant savings compared to retail prices.
  • Distribution: Fresh items are divided among members at convenient pickup locations or through local delivery options.
